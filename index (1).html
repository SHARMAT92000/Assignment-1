Sonu Nigam

Nigam known as one of the most versatile singers with one of the widest vocal ranges in the history of Indian music.Born July 30, 1973, in Faridabad, Haryana, India.

  • Extensive Songography: Nigam has recorded over 6,000 songs in over 32 languages.
  • Versatility: He is known for his versatility, singing in diverse genres and languages, including Hindi, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, and more.
  • Popular Tracks: He has delivered numerous hit tracks like "Main Hoon Na," "Mere Haath Mein," and "Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna,"
  • Non-Film Albums: Besides playback singing, he has also released non-film albums and acted in some Hindi films.
