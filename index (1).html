Sonu Nigam

Legendary Indian Playback Singer

About Sonu Nigam

Sonu Nigam is one of the most versatile and celebrated playback singers in India. Known for his soulful voice and range, he has sung in multiple languages and captivated audiences globally. His career spans over three decades with numerous hits in Bollywood and independent music.

Career Highlights

  • Debut Album: Rafi Ki Yaadein
  • Hit Songs: Kal Ho Naa Ho, Abhi Mujh Mein Kahin, Sandese Aate Hain
  • Winner of multiple Filmfare and National Awards
  • Global concerts and performances across continents
